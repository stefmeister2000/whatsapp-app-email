Automatic follow-up campaigns

Each campaign targets a list/category of leads (or everyone who's messaged you) and sends its own message on its own cadence to people who've gone quiet. A reply from a contact pauses their follow-ups for that campaign; the counter restarts when they write again. Contacts with an open (unresolved) escalation are skipped until it's resolved. Checked every 30 minutes.
Same 24-hour rule applies: follow-ups deliver reliably only with an approved template for older chats. Failed attempts are logged on the server, never spammed twice.
